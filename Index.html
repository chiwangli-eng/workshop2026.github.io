March 26, 2026
09:00 AM - 04:30 PM
Tamkang University

Fostering International Academic Excellence

The Department of Water Resources and Environmental Engineering (TKU) is proud to host the visiting delegation from the Faculty of Agriculture, Natural Resources and Environment at Naresuan University (NU).

Hosted by Prof. Chi-Wang Li and co-led by the visiting delegation head, Prof. Pongsak Lek Noophan, this half-day workshop serves as a premier platform for faculty and post-graduate students to exchange innovative research ideas, focusing on the intersection of water resource management, agricultural sustainability, and environmental resilience.
